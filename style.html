Creativity is the key to Innovation

Creativity :

Creativity is the novel step of being the first
to identify that something might be possible in the first place.

Innovation :

Innovation is the action of putting things into
practical reality, despite challenges and resistance, rather than just contemplating.

A new idea might be novel but it doesn't always bring success. To be relevant, the new idea needs to be considered useful and valuable. The New Coke was a failure because the new formula was not as valued as the original.

Innovations change the way the world operates. Look at iPhones and applications (apps). Innovation is generally disruptive, revolutionary and evolutionary. Society determines what is innovative.
